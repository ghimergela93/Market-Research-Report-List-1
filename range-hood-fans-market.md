<p><p>The range hood fan market includes various types designed to enhance kitchen ventilation. Wall-chimney hoods are installed against walls, providing efficient airflow while complementing kitchen design. Under-cabinet hoods fit snugly under cabinets, maximizing space in smaller kitchens. Island hoods are mounted above kitchen islands, offering powerful ventilation and style in open layouts. Downdraft hoods are integrated into cooktops, retracting when not in use, making them ideal for minimalist designs. Each type caters to different kitchen styles and functional needs.</p></p>
