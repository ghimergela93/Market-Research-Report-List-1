<p><p>Bioprocess Integrity Testing Systems are essential for ensuring the safety and efficacy of biopharmaceutical products. The market is classified into two main types: destructive testing and non-destructive testing. Destructive testing involves methods that examine the integrity of a filter or other bioprocess components but lead to their alteration or destruction. In contrast, non-destructive testing preserves the integrity of the components being tested, allowing for continuous use while still verifying their performance and reliability in bioprocessing applications.</p></p>
