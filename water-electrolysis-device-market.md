<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water electrolysis device market primarily comprises two types: traditional alkaline electrolysers and proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolysers. Traditional alkaline electrolysers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte, offering lower costs and robust durability, making them suitable for large-scale hydrogen production. In contrast, PEM electrolysers use a solid polymer membrane, resulting in higher efficiency and faster response times, ideal for applications demanding rapid hydrogen generation and flexibility. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial and renewable energy integration needs.</p></p>
