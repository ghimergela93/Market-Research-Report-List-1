<p><ul><li>Membrane Separation</li><li>PSA</li><li>Water Scrubber</li><li>Chemical Scrubber</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biogas upgrading systems enhance the quality of biogas by removing impurities and separating methane from other gases. Key market types include:</p><p>1. **Membrane Separation**: Utilizes selective membranes to separate methane from carbon dioxide and other gases.</p><p>2. **Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA)**: Employs a series of adsorption and desorption cycles under varying pressures to concentrate methane.</p><p>3. **Water Scrubber**: Uses water to absorb carbon dioxide and other impurities, allowing purified methane to be collected.</p><p>4. **Chemical Scrubber**: Involves chemical reactions to remove specific contaminants from biogas.</p><p>5. **Others**: Incorporates emerging technologies and hybrid systems for biogas upgrading.</p></p>
