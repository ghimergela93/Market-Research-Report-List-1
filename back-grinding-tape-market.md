<p><p>Back grinding tape is essential in semiconductor manufacturing for wafer thinning processes. The market is segmented into UV curable and non-UV curable types. UV curable back grinding tape cures when exposed to ultraviolet light, offering advantages like quick processing and strong adhesion, making it suitable for advanced applications. Non-UV curable tape relies on thermal or pressure-based curing methods, which may offer flexibility but generally requires longer processing times. Both types cater to specific production needs and technology requirements in the industry.</p></p>
