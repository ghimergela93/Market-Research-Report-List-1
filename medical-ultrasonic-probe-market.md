<p><p>The medical ultrasonic probe market comprises several key types based on their design and application. Linear type probes offer high-resolution imaging for superficial structures, making them ideal for vascular and musculoskeletal examinations. Convex type probes provide wider scanning angles, suitable for abdominal assessments. Phased array type probes are compact and versatile, often used in cardiac imaging due to their ability to produce real-time images. Endocavitary probes are specialized for internal examinations, particularly in gynecological and urological contexts, enhancing diagnostic precision.</p></p>
