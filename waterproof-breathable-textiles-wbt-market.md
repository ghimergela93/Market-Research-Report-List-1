<p><p>Waterproof Breathable Textiles (WBT) are specialized fabrics designed to repel water while allowing moisture vapor to escape, ensuring comfort during strenuous activities. The market is primarily segmented into two types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) and EPFE (Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene). PTFE is known for its excellent waterproofing and durability, making it popular in outdoor gear. EPFE, with enhanced breathability and flexibility, is often used in high-performance applications. Both materials cater to diverse sectors, including outdoor apparel, medical textiles, and industrial applications.</p></p>
