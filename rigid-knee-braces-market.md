<p><strong>The Rigid Knee Braces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prophylactic Braces</li><li>Functional Braces</li><li>Rehabilitative Braces</li><li>Unloader Braces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid knee braces market encompasses four main types: prophylactic braces, designed to prevent injuries during sports; functional braces, which provide support for knees with ligament damage during rehabilitation; rehabilitative braces, aimed at facilitating recovery after surgery or injury by limiting motion; and unloader braces, specifically used to relieve pressure on the affected knee compartments, often for osteoarthritis patients. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying medical, athletic, and recovery needs, enhancing knee stability and functionality.</p></p>
