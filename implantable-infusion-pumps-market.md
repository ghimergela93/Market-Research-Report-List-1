<p><p>The implantable infusion pumps market features various types, primarily including piston type and creeping type infusion pumps. Piston type infusion pumps utilize a piston mechanism to deliver precise medication dosages, ensuring accurate flow rates. In contrast, creeping type infusion pumps rely on a peristaltic mechanism, allowing flexible and gentle medication delivery. Both types offer advantages in maintaining patient comfort and improving therapeutic outcomes, catering to distinct clinical needs in areas such as pain management and chronic disease treatment.</p></p>
