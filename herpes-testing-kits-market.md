<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Herpes Testing Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody/Antigen Based Kit</li><li>Viral Culture Test Kits</li><li>Nucleic Acid Amplification-based Kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Herpes testing kits comprise several types, including antibody/antigen-based kits, viral culture test kits, and nucleic acid amplification-based kits. Antibody/antigen kits detect the immune response to herpes simplex virus (HSV) or the virus itself, while viral culture test kits isolate and identify live virus from samples, primarily during an active outbreak. Nucleic acid amplification-based kits, such as PCR tests, identify the genetic material of the virus, providing rapid and sensitive detection of HSV. Each type serves distinct diagnostic needs and scenarios.</p></p>
