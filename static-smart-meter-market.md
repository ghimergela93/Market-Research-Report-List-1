<p><p>The static smart meter market is divided into two main types: Direct Current (DC) meters and Alternating Current (AC) meters. DC meters are designed for measuring direct current, commonly used in renewable energy systems like solar panels and battery storage, ensuring accurate energy consumption tracking. In contrast, AC meters are predominantly used in residential and commercial settings, measuring energy supplied by the grid. Both types enhance energy management, support real-time monitoring, and facilitate integration with smart grid technologies.</p></p>
