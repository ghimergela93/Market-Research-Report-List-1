<p><p>The Desktop Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine market is segmented into two main types: Contact and Non-Contact systems. Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machines utilize tactile probes to physically touch the surface, providing precise measurements of roughness and contours. In contrast, Non-Contact Measuring Machines employ optical methods, such as laser scanning or white light interferometry, to capture surface characteristics without direct contact, making them suitable for delicate or highly reflective materials. Both types serve critical roles in quality control and surface analysis across various industries.</p></p>
