<p><p>Cyber-Physical Production Systems (CPPS) encompass two primary market types: EP-CPS (Embedded Physical CPS) and IT-CPS (Information Technology CPS). EP-CPS focuses on integrating embedded systems with physical processes, allowing for real-time monitoring and control of manufacturing operations. In contrast, IT-CPS emphasizes the use of information technologies, such as big data and cloud computing, to optimize production through data analytics and connectivity. Together, these markets enhance automation, efficiency, and responsiveness in smart manufacturing environments.</p></p>
