<p><p>The buccal tubes market can be categorized into two main types: smooth bottom and net bottom buccal tubes. Smooth bottom buccal tubes feature a sleek, flat design, allowing for easier placement and optimal alignment of orthodontic archwires. In contrast, net bottom buccal tubes incorporate a mesh-like structure, providing enhanced retention and stability during orthodontic treatments. Both designs cater to different orthodontic needs, impacting treatment efficiency and patient comfort in various clinical scenarios.</p></p>
